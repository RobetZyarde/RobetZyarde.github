boring

Disturbed by myself, I found it time for me to vent depressed mood.
I was busy requesting for notarization at this time last year.
My mother and I traveled around Changzhou and at that time I had not haven any ideas about my future.
Tired and gloomy, I turned into silence and unfriendness because of this uncertainty.
Whether should I leave this country? Whether will I be accepted by the USA?
Is this a good choice or not? I haven’t considered this way before at all.
Now, still trapped in depression, I can not blame my mood on the uncertain future.
It is obvious that I need to spend time solving my personal affairs such as TOFEL, PS and so on,
but I still felt worried and I became versatile and irritable, which may be called immature.
Someone may say that this is a process of growth,
as a result I will become mature and all of those yesterdays will vanish from sight.
Maybe they are right. Maybe another reason is that these situations are not difficulties at all.
I am just lucky and talented, who have well education and this can be called welfare.
Thanks to the giving of everyone other than any other unexistent, the weak man became liberal and strong.
Unfortunately, he still wants to fuck the world just like bojack horseman dut to his tininess.
I seemed to be little stubborn and paid too much useless hope on those things I can’t control.
Do hope someone offer me a more wonderful and more interesting life?
Become simple merely.
We are all going wrong.

谁不希望啊
